Video Auto Uploader Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-15

1. Overview

Video Auto Uploader ("the App") is a video scheduling and publishing tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: the username you register with the App.
  • Connected platform tokens: when you connect a third-party account (such as TikTok) via that platform's official OAuth login, we store the access token and refresh token issued to us, so we can publish content on your behalf, only when you request it.
  • Video files: videos you upload are stored temporarily on our server for the sole purpose of publishing them to the platforms you select, and are deleted immediately after publishing completes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information above solely to operate the App: authenticating you, publishing the content you submit to the platforms you have connected, and displaying upload status/results back to you.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share your data with third parties, except with the platform(s) you have explicitly connected (e.g. TikTok), and only to perform the publishing actions you request.

5. Data Retention

Connected-account tokens are retained until you disconnect the account or delete your App account. Uploaded video files are deleted from our server immediately after processing.

6. Your Rights

You may disconnect any connected third-party account at any time, both from within the App and from that platform's own account/app settings. You may also request deletion of your App account and associated data by contacting us (see below).

7. Security

Access tokens are stored on our server and are not exposed to other users of the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to starmaru@naver.com.
