Privacy Policy

When a user connects a third-party account (such as TikTok) through the official OAuth login flow, we store the access/refresh token needed to publish content on that user's behalf, at that user's request. Tokens are used only to perform the actions the user initiates in the app (such as uploading a video) and are not shared with third parties. A user may revoke access at any time from within the app or from the third-party platform's own account settings. Last updated: 2026-07-15
